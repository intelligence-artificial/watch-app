# Japanese Futuristic Watch Faces

## What
Added 3 futuristic Japanese-style watch faces to the `watch_faces` module.

## Watch Faces

### 漢字ネオン (Kanji Neon)
- Cyberpunk digital clock with kanji numerals (一二三...)
- Neon cyan/pink glow, hex grid background, scan lines
- Second progress arc, Japanese date format, AM/PM (午前/午後)

### 東京ドリフト (Tokyo Drift)
- Analog face inspired by rainy Tokyo nights
- Animated falling rain streaks, pulsing neon arc segments
- Neon red hour hand with glow, Japanese day-of-week kanji (月火水...)

### 桜回路 (Sakura Circuit)
- Zen meets technology — circuit traces bloom like cherry blossoms
- 60 circuit segments illuminate progressively with seconds
- Floating sakura petal particles, clean monospace digital time

- `KanjiNeonWatchFaceService.kt`

- `SakuraCircuitWatchFaceService.kt`
